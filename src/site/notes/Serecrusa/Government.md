## Tricameral Parliament




> [!Info|right] President
>###### *Pirmininkaujanti*
> Represented as Head of State, they would take matters in foreign affairs, its a more independent and powerful position, being elected by a popular vote. Their term would usually last 10 years.
>###### Prime Minister
>###### *Ministras Pirmininkas*
>The Prime Minister is represented as the Head of Government, acting as Vice President and overseeing the President’s ministry. This position would typically be appointed by the President. They would take matters in domestic affairs such as implementing laws, and they can be voted out of office by the legislature via a vote of no confidence.

>[!info] Regioninis Senatas
>![Pasted image 20250908170345.png|left](/img/user/Images/Pasted%20image%2020250908170345.png)
>The Regioninis Senatas is one of three legislatures within the Serecrusian government, made of all the regional governors of the homeworld Sera. They would be primarily focused on laws addressing a wide range of issues on their homeworld. There are about 208 seats in total. The Regioninis Senatas is overseen by the Homeworld Orator. Voting would simply be down with a 2/3rds majority, typically 104 seats.

>[!info] Sektorių Senatas
>![Pasted image 20250908170809.png|left](/img/user/Images/Pasted%20image%2020250908170809.png)
>The Sektorių Senatas is the second of three legislatures within the Serecrusian government, made of all the planetary & sector governors. They would focus on matters that concern the outer sectors that would not affect the core sector. There would be 65 seats in total. They would be overseen by the Soverign Orator.
