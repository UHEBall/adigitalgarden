## Overview

Most of the [[Serecrusa/Zmoneians\|Zmoneians]] would follow a spiritualistic religion known as **Vienism**, with the remaining either being agnostic or atheist. Its widely believed that Vienism is part of the Ethnoreligious Apaksejan group, as the Apaksejans are synonymous with their religion, and as is the remaining religion, its pluralistic philosophy allows the belief system to be easily practiced & widespread.
The spiritual goal within Vienism is to become one with the Vienas through prayer, discipline, and following monastic rules or Vienist morality, such as kindness, restraint, and compassion for others. More conservative sects would adhere to a monastic lifestyle, while more centrist sects adhere to basic moral rules.
### Animism & Ancestor Worship
Vienism asserts that essential aspects of nature such as wildlife & plantlife withhold **Dvasia**, a spirit that is said to possess past lives, and because of this principle, when the average intelligent being dies, they are to be buried back into the soil, and venerated.
### Pantheism
Another core aspect of Vienism is the concept of **Vienas**, which is recognized within the religion, and thus the name. It asserts that the universe itself is the ultimate reality & holds a god-like essence in the cosmos. In Vienism, its interpretation of the universe is divided into three realms, the **Visata** (material universe), the **Dangu**s (The inner heavens) and the **Eteris** (The outer heavens)
